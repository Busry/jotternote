Procedure
Spinal anesthesia was administered and patient placed in lithotomy position. The perineum and vagina were cleaned and draped in sterile fashion.
A circumferential incision was made around the cervix. The bladder was dissected off the cervix and the anterior and posterior pouches opened. The uterosacral and cardinal ligaments were clamped, cut and ligated, followed by the uterine vessels. The uterus was delivered vaginally and removed.
Haemostasis was secured. The vaginal vault was closed with absorbable sutures.
Anterior colporrhaphy was performed to repair the cystocele by plication of the pubocervical fascia.
Posterior colporrhaphy was performed to repair the rectocele by plication of the rectovaginal fascia.
Perineorrhaphy was done to reconstruct the perineal body.
Haemostasis confirmed. Vaginal pack inserted and Foley catheter inserted
